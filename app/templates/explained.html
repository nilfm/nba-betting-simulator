What is this?

The idea

NBA Betting Simulator is a website that allows you to simulate bets on real NBA games. You start with 1000 coins that you can bet on the daily NBA games, with (almost) real-time odds. You can reset your account at any time and it is completely free to play.

How do the odds work?

If you bet X coins on a game with odds Y and you win, your net benefit is X*(Y-1) coins.

As an example, if you bet 50 coins on a game with odds 1.32 and you win, you receive 50*1.32 = 66 coins. That means your net benefit is 16 coins.
